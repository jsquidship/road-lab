The ROAD Lab

How motivation and opportunity shape the roads we take through adulthood.

The ROAD Lab investigates how motivation develops within opportunities and constraints across adulthood, shaping and responding to trajectories in work, relationships, health, and social mobility.

Directed by Dr. Jacob Shane, Associate Professor, Brooklyn College & The Graduate Center, CUNY
