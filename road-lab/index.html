About the Lab

The ROAD Lab at Brooklyn College investigates how individuals navigate major life transitions, balance competing goals, and sustain motivation in the face of uncertainty. We draw on lifespan developmental theory to understand the dynamic interplay between personal motivation, social relationships, and structural opportunity across adulthood.

Meet Dr. Jacob Shane

Principal Investigator & Associate Professor

Jacob Shane, Ph.D., is an Associate Professor in the Department of Psychology at Brooklyn College and the CUNY Graduate Center. His research examines how individuals maintain motivation and adapt goal pursuit strategies across major life transitions. He holds a Ph.D. from UC Irvine (2014), an MSc from Keele University (2007), and a BA from UC Boulder (2002). Jake is passionate about mentoring the next generation of developmental psychologists and creating inclusive spaces in science.
