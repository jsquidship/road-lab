Principal Investigator

JS

Dr. Jacob Shane, Ph.D.

Associate Professor, Department of Psychology

Brooklyn College, City University of New York

Google Scholar ResearchGate Faculty Page

Jacob Shane holds a Ph.D. from UC Irvine (2014, advisor: Jutta Heckhausen), an MSc from Keele University (2007), and a BA from UC Boulder (2002). His research examines how individuals maintain motivation and adapt goal pursuit strategies across major life transitions, with particular focus on work, retirement, socioeconomic attainment, and close relationships.

Jake is deeply committed to mentoring students at all levels and creating inclusive, supportive research communities. He believes that the best science happens when diverse perspectives are welcomed, when questions are driven by real-world human concerns, and when students are given agency in shaping their own scholarly journeys. Outside the lab, Jake is an avid hiker, a hobbyist chef, and a lifelong learner of languages.

Our Lab Culture

The ROAD Lab is a collaborative, supportive research community where everyone's voice matters. We believe in:

  • Intellectual Curiosity. We ask questions that matter to real people's lives. We read widely and think deeply.
  • Rigor and Integrity. We hold ourselves to the highest standards of methodological rigor and research ethics.
  • Mentorship. Lab leaders are deeply invested in your growth as a scholar, researcher, and person.
  • Inclusion. We actively recruit and support students from underrepresented backgrounds in psychology and academia.
  • Work-Life Balance. We recognize that good science comes from people who are well and fulfilled.
  • Collaborative Spirit. We celebrate each other's successes and support each other through challenges.
